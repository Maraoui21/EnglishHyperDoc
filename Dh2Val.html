GEIW Evaluation

On March 16th and 17th, 2023, the ENSET Mohammedia School hosted a hackathon with the theme "Blockchain and AI for health." Participants formed groups and were tasked with developing digital solutions that integrate these technologies into healthcare. The hackathon provided opportunities for learning new skills, collaborating with peers, and showcasing creativity and problem-solving abilities. Mentors and industry experts were available to support participants throughout the event, and a panel of judges evaluated the projects based on various criteria. After two days of intense collaboration, the winning team was announced. Their solution demonstrated innovative use of both blockchain and AI to improve healthcare delivery and management. The team received recognition and support for further developing their solution. The hackathon was a successful event that encouraged innovation and collaboration while addressing real-world healthcare challenges using cutting-edge technologies. The ENSET Mohammedia School plans to continue hosting similar hackathons in the future to foster creativity, problem-solving skills, and the development of practical solutions in the health sector.

DH2 Evaluation

"Diversifying Horizons" is a conference organized by GLSID 2 and BDCC 2 students that is focused on engineering students' careers. The conference features Ahmed JAAFARI, Sofia EL BAHJA, and Ayman LAFAZ as guest speakers, who share their experiences and insights on what to do after completing engineering studies. The conference provides valuable information for engineering students looking to diversify their career horizons.
